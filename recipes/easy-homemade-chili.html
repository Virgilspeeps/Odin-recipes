Easy Homemade Chili

A homemade chili crowd pleaser!

Homemade chili is delicious and goes great with cornbread or over corn chips for a chili pie! I like to use spicy pinto beans. This chili is loaded with beef, beans, and crowd-pleasing flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1(15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1(14.5 oz) can kidney beans
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 pinch chili powder
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. 1. Place ground beef and onion in a large saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir until meat is browned and onion is tender, about 5 to 7 minutes
  2. 2. Stir in tomato sauce, kidney beans, stewed tomatoes with juice, and water. Season with chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover and let simmer for 15 minutes.
